ContractMissions

ContractMissions brings dynamic, replayable contract jobs to GTA V, offering a fast-paced mix of heists, chases, ambushes, raids, and high-risk takedowns. Every contract spawns naturally around the map and can be triggered instantly by the player, creating a seamless experience that feels integrated into the world of Los Santos.

Take on VIP assassinations, highway hijacks, gang territory raids, cargo truck robberies, special destruction contracts, and more. ContractMissions includes eight unique mission types, each with its own gameplay loop. Whether you want new content, more chaos, or a reason to return to Story Mode, ContractMissions delivers a full contract-style experience with endless replay value.
